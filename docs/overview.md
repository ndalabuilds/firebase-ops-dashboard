# Overview

## Goal

Show how a small business can use a Firebase-powered internal dashboard to manage core operational workflows.

## Problem

Many businesses handle operations across too many places:

- spreadsheets
- email threads
- manual status updates
- disconnected notes
- unclear ownership of tasks or leads

This creates friction and poor visibility.

## Proposed solution

A lightweight Firebase ops dashboard can centralize operational data and provide a simple internal interface.

It can:

- display business metrics
- store and update operational records
- track statuses
- support role-based access
- reduce manual admin work

## Example scenario

A business receives new leads and wants to track them through qualification, follow-up, and onboarding.

The dashboard can show:

- total leads
- new leads
- leads in progress
- converted leads
- overdue follow-ups

It can also allow team members to update statuses and review records in one place.
